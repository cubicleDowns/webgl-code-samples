Game Dimensions:

This demo uses THREE.js rays and ray casters to determin win conditions and to calculate the computers next turn.

The ray approach works for N-N-N dimenions of tic-tac-toe. I've clamped the number of dimensions to 10.

If you pick 10, you'll need to connect 10 in a row.
